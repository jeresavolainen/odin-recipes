Lasagna

Warm and cheesy Lasagna is the perfect meal to your dinner table.

Lasagna is traditional Italian dish. It is made of layered pasta sheets, bolognese sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna pasta sheets

Meat sauce

  • 400 grams beef with at least 10% fat
  • 3 onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoons oil
  • 400 grams chopped tomatoes
  • 1 dl water
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper

Cheese filling

  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 3 tablespoons wheat flour
  • 6 dl milk
  • 150 grams shredded cheese (this recipe uses emmental)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper

On top

  • 1 dl shredded cheese

Instructions

Meat sauce

  1. Drizzle some oil to frying pan and cook meat, diced onions and garlic.

Cheese filling

  1. Heat up the oil and add flour.
  2. Add milk gradually and mix until smooth. Turn up the heat, add in the cheese and mix continuously. Add spices.

Lasagna

  1. Take a deep dish and oil it. Add a layer of meat sauce so it covers the bottom, then add a layer of cheese filling. Stack 3 pasta sheets on top of the layers. Repeat two times.
  2. After all layers and complete, give them little a push to ensure sauce spread evenly. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  3. Cook in 175 celsius for about an hour. Allow to rest for 15-20 minutes before serving.
