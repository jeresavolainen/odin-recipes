Stroganoff

Creamy Stroganoff served with potatoes in a bowl

Traditionally Stroganoff gets it's rich flavour from onions, tomatoes and pickle cubes.

Ingredients

  • 600 grams beef roast
  • 30 grams butter.
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 4 dl water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 120 grams sour cream
  • 1-2 tablespoon thickening agent
  • 2 Russian pickles
  • Parsley for garnishing
  1. Cut the meat into cubes. Cook meat cubes in butter with frying pan until brown, you should do this in 2 or 3 parts. Transfer meat cubes into a pan. Saut√© diced onion and tomato paste on the frying pan for a brief moment, then add into pan with meat.
  2. Add water, salt, pepper and mustard into the pan. Let simmer on low heat for an hour, until fully cooked.
  3. Add sour cream, turn up the heat until boiling. Add thickening agent into boiling sauce. Mix. Let it boil for 2-3 minutes. Lastly add pickle cubes.
  4. Optionally add finely chopped parsley for garnish.
