Finnish pea soup

It's not difficult to make Finnish pea soup, but it is time consuming. This delicious dish is worth the time.

Finnish pea soup is delicious and economical dish. It's deeply rooted in Finnish culture, it is usually eaten thursdays and paired with Finnish pancake. To get the authentic experience, try having your pea soup with mustard and diced onions.

Ingredients

  • 400 grams dry peas
  • 2 liters waters
  • 600 grams ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2,5 dl cream
  • 1 tablespoon marjoram

Instructions

  1. Wash the peas under running water and set into pot. Add water and let soak overnight.
  2. Set the pot on stove and turn up the heat. Add meat in small shreds and salt to the pot. Let boil for 2 hours, while occasionally stirring making sure soup doesn't get burnt. Add cream when the soup starts to thickening and let it thicken to your liking.
  3. Taste and add salt if needed.
